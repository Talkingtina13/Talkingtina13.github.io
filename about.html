Our Start

Hi I'm Christina and along side me is my favorite furry friend Forest. Our Journey started about 2 years ago when I had seen a sign for Border Colllie puppies on my way home from work. Like any young college student I had no impulse form-control and decided I needed a new memeber to my family. However like many I didnt do the early research for my pup and went through a rollercoaster of emotions with my little guy from potty accidents to nibbled floor boards and holey socks.Hopefulluy with my site you can learn a few tips and tricks to save you the headache.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

Our mission at City Pup Tips is to help you educate, train and find local businesses that have the best intrest of your dogs in mind.

You may come from a city where resources are nonexistent or in an area where there is too many to choose from with our site we hope to help you find what is best for you by teaming up with vendors who understand a new member takes time to adjust and want to help make the transtion go as smoothly as possible.
