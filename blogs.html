Keeping Your Dog Busy While at Work

We all wish we could hang out with our pups 24/7, but unfortunately, we have to work! Being gone for long hours during the day can be boring for dogs and stressful for owners while we wonder what our furry friend is up to while we're. A simple way to keep your dog occupied while at work is to rotate their toys regularly. This is a cost-efficient solution because it won't require you to buy more toys, instead limits your pup's access to the ones they already have. Gather all your pup’s toys and choose a few for them to have at the beginning of the week. Every few days, swap those out for others from your dog’s collection. This hack ensures your dog doesn’t get bored with their toys quickly.

Aside from switching out their toys, there have been numerous studies that show playing classical music with dogs can reduce bad behaviors that arise with anxiety and stress, and increase relaxation. So next time you need to leave for a few hours, try a little Classic FM or youtube playing for your pup to enjoy?
