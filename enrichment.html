Why is Enrichment Essential

The purpose of enrichment is to reduce stress in dogs from being lonely and at home all day. Providing a dog with enrichment allows your pet to have a sense of control over their environment, which can help to reduce and avoid stress. By allowing them to engage in their innate behaviors, such as playing, chasing, smelling, chewing and scavenging you let them to be physically, emotionally and mentally satisfied.
