Enrichment

Why is Enrichment Essential

The purpose of enrichment is to reduce stress in your pets from being home all day. Providing a dog with enrichment lets your dog have a sense of control over their life. By allowing them to engage in playing, chasing, smelling, chewing, and scavenging you let them be physically, emotionally, and mentally stimulated. Dogs who don’t receive stimulation tend to find ways to enrich themselves, resulting in unwanted rully behaviors.
