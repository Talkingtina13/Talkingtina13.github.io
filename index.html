Keeping Your Dog Busy While at Work

A simple way to keep your dog distracted while you’re at work is to rotate their toys regularly. This is a cost-efficient solution because it won't require you to buy more toys, instead limits your pup's access to the ones they already have. Gather all your pup’s toys and choose a few for them to have at the beginning of the week. Every few days, swap those out for others from your dog’s collection. This hack ensures your dog doesn’t get bored with their toys quickly.
