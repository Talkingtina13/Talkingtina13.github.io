What is Dog Socialization?

Dog socialization is the process of making your dog feel comfortable in the presence of other dogs, new people, environments and situations. It is all about your fluff ball gaining experiences. Understanding the world helps your dog live a happier, more relaxed life.

It’s easier to socialize puppies, because they are naturally fearless and curious.They haven’t a chance developed any negative feelings toward people, places or other animals.The ideal time frame for socialization, is seven weeks to four months. After this time frame it is still possible but the road will be much longer

To get your pup socialized before their shots take them along to see, smell, and experience new people and places. Allow your dog to explore around the playground, climb up and downplay equipment, meet different people and of course, let them play with other vacinated dogs as well!

you should also practice sound exposure as some noises are scary to pups, it may be the vacuum cleaner, a truck passing by or even fireworks. Exposing your dogs to different sounds is an important part of the socialization process and you need to expose them to sounds that may be scary.
