Crate Training

Why You Need Crate Training

As a training tool, crates are a great way to potty train, set up house rules and manners, and give your dog a safe zone.Dogs can retreat to their crates when situations are too chaotic or scary. It also helps dogs confront new situations successfully, like the addition of a new baby having company over.
